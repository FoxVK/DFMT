Error codes returned by most of functions.

Enumerator
LIBDFMT_OK 

No error.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_TIMEOUT 

Timeour.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_TUNER_BUSY 

CTS bit != 1 or tuner is alraday performing I2C communication.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_TUNER_ERROR 

ERR bit = 1.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_DEVICE_ERROR 

Device received not enough data to recognize what to do.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_DEVICE_DISCONNECTED 

Device is not present.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_DEVICE_INTERNAL 

Hardware or firmware malfunction.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_USB_ERROR 

Libusb error, probably more than one instaces is running.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_BAD_ARGUMENT 

Argument of invalid value or out of range.

LIBDFMT_ERROR_NO_PERMISSION 

You have insufficient permissions to open device. (probably missing udev rules on linux)

LIBDFMT_DEVICE_CLOSED 

Device is not opened or was closed.

LIBDFMT_ERROR 

General error.
